How It All Started

It all started when Nathan needed a change in his career. As an engineer who worked in a coffee manufacturing plant, he developed the necessary experience to make great coffee. Together, Nathan and his best friend and business partner, Paul, who also comes from an engineering background, with their mutual love and passion for coffee, decided to pack their bags and move to Vancouver in 2012 to start a coffee shop, where hipster coffee shops and bars dominate the landscape.

In just 6 years, with their hard work and satisfaction from bringing a smile to the customers' faces, today, they have expanded to 3 locations.






Coffee and Food Quality

At Nathan's Coffee, we believe quality is of the utmost importance to guarantee satisfaction from our customers. We use the highest quality arabica beans from Ethiopia. All our coffee beans are fair trade and the workers are fairly compensated for their hard work.

Our food is certified organic, gluten free, and we have numerous vegan options for our meat-unfriendly customers. We do not have any preservatives in our food, our bread is baked fresh daily, and coffee beans are all within 6 months old otherwise we throw it away. We believe in locally sourced food, and besides the coffee, because we know Canadians are horrible at growing coffee beans, everything else besides the coffee is produced within 100 kilometers from the store.
